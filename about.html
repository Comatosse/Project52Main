Our Journey

2018

The Foundation

What started as a small group of passionate engineers and educators grew into a movement. Project52 was established with a clear vision: to make robotics education accessible to all.

  • First robotics workshop conducted
  • Partnership with 3 local schools
  • Initial team of 5 mentors formed
2019

First Competition Success

Our dedication paid off as we hosted our first regional robotics competition, bringing together teams from across the state.

  • 12 schools participated
  • Over 200 students involved
  • Featured in local media
2020

Digital Transformation

Adapting to global challenges, we pioneered virtual robotics education and remote competitions, reaching more students than ever before.

  • Launched online learning platform
  • Virtual competition platform developed
  • 500+ students participated remotely
2021

Global Expansion

Project52 went international, forming partnerships with organizations worldwide and establishing our first overseas chapters.

  • Expanded to 5 countries
  • International mentorship program launched
  • First global virtual competition
